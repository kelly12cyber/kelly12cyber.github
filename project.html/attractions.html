ATTRACTIONS AND LANDMARKS

Four well-known tourist sites in Ghana, their locations, and what visitors can see or do there:

1. Cape Coast Castle

Location: Cape Coast, Central Region

What visitors can see:

  • A historic slave trade castle built by Europeans
  • Museums and guided tours about the trans-Atlantic slave trade
  • Dungeons, courtyards, and ocean views

2. Kakum National Park

Location: Near Cape Coast, Central Region

What visitors can do:

  • Walk on the famous canopy walkway high above the forest
  • Explore tropical rainforest scenery
  • See birds, butterflies, and other wildlife

3. Mole National Park

Location: Damango, Savannah Region

What visitors can see:

  • Ghana’s largest wildlife park
  • Elephants, antelopes, baboons, warthogs, and birds
  • Guided safari walks and jeep tours

4. Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park

Location: Accra, Greater Accra Region

What visitors can see:

  • Mausoleum of Dr. Kwame Nkrumah (Ghana’s first President)
  • Museum with photos, artifacts, and independence history
  • Beautiful gardens and fountains
